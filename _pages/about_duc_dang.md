**Dr. Duc Ngoc Minh Dang** is a seasoned researcher and educator with expertise spanning wireless networks, deep learning, and Internet of Things.

## Education & Career

He received the B.Eng and M.Eng degrees in Telecommunications Engineering from Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology, Vietnam, in 2005 and 2007, respectively. He completed his Ph.D. in Computer Engineering at Kyung Hee University, Korea, in 2014.

**Professional Experience:**
- 2005-2008: Senior Telecom Engineer at TMA Solutions, Vietnam
- 2008-2011: Head of Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering Department at Ton Duc Thang University, Vietnam
- 2011-2014: Ph.D. assistant and post-doc researcher at Kyung Hee University, Korea
- 2015-2022: Vice Dean, School of Graduate Studies at Ton Duc Thang University, Vietnam
- 2022-Present: Researcher at FPT University, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam

## Research Interests

- MAC protocols in Wireless Ad hoc Networks
- Vehicular Ad hoc Networks (VANETs)
- Deep Learning
- Internet of Things (IoT)

He was the **Valedictorian in Artificial Intelligence** from FPT University, Vietnam, and is now pursuing advanced studies in integrative biotechnology with a focus on deep learning approaches for biological data.

## expertise & Strengths

Nguyen excels in:
- Research design and methodology
- Software coding and implementation
- Proposing novel research ideas
- Computational and deep learning methods

## Research Interests

- Computational Biology
- Bioinformatics
- Deep Learning

## Long-term Goal

His long-term goal is to develop innovative deep learning frameworks that contribute meaningfully to computational biology and advance the field through novel computational approaches.

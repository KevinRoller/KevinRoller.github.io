**Mr. Duong Thanh Tran** (also known as Jayden) is a Ph.D. student in Integrative Biotechnology at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), Korea, and a full-time researcher in the Computational Biology and Bioinformatics Laboratory.

## Education & Current Position

He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. at the Department of Integrative Biotechnology, College of Biotechnology and Bioengineering, Sungkyunkwan University, Republic of Korea. He works under the guidance of Professor Balachandran Manavalan in the Computational Biology and Bioinformatics Laboratory (CBBL-SKKU).

## Research Interests

- Deep Learning
- Bioinformatics
- Computer Vision
- Natural Language Processing
- Explainable AI
- Multimodal Learning
